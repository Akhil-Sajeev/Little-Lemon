Little Lemon

To the taste that brings back moments

Based in Bengaluru , Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist. The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, Turkish culture, Indian and have flavour from different parts of the world and have a menu of 12–15 items that they rotate seasonally.

Continental cuisine

Encompasses a diverse range of culinary traditions from Europe and beyond. From the rustic flavors of Italian pasta dishes to the sophisticated techniques of French haute cuisine, this category celebrates the art of cooking across borders.

relies on fresh, high-quality ingredients. Staples include wheat, olive oil, butter, cheese, and herbs. Expect a harmonious blend of savory, sweet, and umami flavors. Herbs like rosemary, thyme, and oregano infuse dishes with aromatic richness.

From slow braising to delicate poaching, chefs emphasize precision and balance.

Greek Foods

deeply rooted in Mediterranean flavors, celebrates simplicity, freshness, and communal dining. From sun-kissed islands to bustling cities, Greece offers a vibrant culinary journey.

Greek cuisine reflects ancient traditions, emphasizing communal feasting, fresh produce, and the joy of sharing meals with loved ones.

Indian Food and Flavors

Indian cuisine is a kaleidoscope of regional diversity, spices, and vibrant colors. From street vendors to royal kitchens, India’s culinary heritage is a sensory explosion.
